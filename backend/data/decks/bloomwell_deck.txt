Bloomwell - Mental health support for new parents

Why me, why now
I spent nine years as a midwife in Vienna. I watched hundreds of new parents
slide into anxiety and postnatal depression with nowhere to turn between the
six-week checkup and a crisis. The system only catches them when it is already
bad.

The problem, specifically
- 1 in 5 new mothers experiences a perinatal mood disorder
- The median wait for specialist support is 11 weeks
- Generic meditation apps ignore the specific triggers of the postpartum period
  (sleep deprivation cycles, feeding guilt, identity loss)

What I am building
A guided companion that adapts to the postpartum timeline week by week, with
screening built from the validated EPDS scale and escalation to a human when
risk crosses a threshold. Not another content library - a triage layer.

Where I am
- Working prototype tested with 8 parents from my former clinic
- No funding, no co-founder yet, no revenue
- Deep relationships with two midwife networks who will refer

What I need
A first check to hire one clinical engineer and run a proper 50-parent study.
