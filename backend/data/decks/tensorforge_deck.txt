TensorForge - Serving layer for cheap, fast LLM inference

The problem
Teams shipping LLM features burn 40-70% of their compute budget on inference.
GPUs sit half-idle because batching and quantization are hard to get right.

Our solution
A drop-in inference runtime. Point your model at TensorForge; we handle
continuous batching, speculative decoding, and INT8/FP8 quantization.
Average measured cost reduction across design partners: 61%.
