HelioHealth - Early sepsis warning from routine vitals

The problem
Sepsis kills 11 million people a year. Detection is late because the signs are
subtle and buried in streaming vitals nurses cannot watch continuously.

Our product
A bedside model that ingests heart rate, temperature, and lab values and raises
a risk score 6 hours before clinical deterioration.

Clinical validation
- FDA 510(k) clearance in progress
- Live in 3 hospital pilots across the NHS
- AUROC 0.89 on retrospective validation (14,000 admissions)
